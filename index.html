Creating Custom Stamps Online with Online Stamp Doc

Welcome to Online Stamp Doc, the premier online tool for generating custom digital stamps, seals, and watermarks. Whether you need a company seal, an "APPROVED" or "CONFIDENTIAL" document marker, or a specialized multi-line business address stamp, Online Stamp Doc provides a powerful, easy-to-use interface to design and apply your stamp in seconds.

How to Use Online Stamp Doc

  1. Design Your Stamp: Choose from circular, rectangular, oval, diamond, or star shapes. Customize the text, fonts, ink color, and borders to match your brand.
  2. Add Effects: Apply a distressed/grungy ink effect for a realistic look, or upload your custom logo to the center of the stamp.
  3. Download or Apply: You can download the high-resolution PNG of your stamp, or proceed to Step 2 to digitally stamp a PDF, JPG, or PNG document right here in your browser.
  4. Export Document: Position and rotate your stamp on the document canvas, then export the finalized file securely.

100% Secure & Private

Your privacy is our top priority. Online Stamp Doc operates entirely within your browser. Your sensitive PDF documents and images are never uploaded to our servers. All rendering, stamping, and exporting happens instantly on your local device, ensuring maximum security for your confidential files.
