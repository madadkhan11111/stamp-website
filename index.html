Free Online Stamp Maker & PDF Stamper

Welcome to Online Stamp Doc, the most versatile tool to design custom stamps online and apply them to your documents. Whether you need an official company seal, a professional business stamp, or a creative watermark, our platform allows you to create high-quality digital stamps for free.

Why Use Our Online Stamp Generator?

Traditional physical stamps can be expensive and slow to produce. With our digital stamp maker, you can design a custom stamp in seconds and use it immediately. Here is why users choose us:

  • No Software Needed: Create and apply stamps directly in your web browser.
  • PDF & Image Support: Stamp PDF documents, JPG images, and PNG files with ease.
  • Fully Customizable: Choose shapes, colors, fonts, and even upload your own logo.
  • Realistic Effects: Add "distress" textures to make your digital stamp look like real ink on paper.

How to Stamp a Document Online

  1. Design: Use our editor to create your stamp. Select from circular, rectangular, or oval shapes.
  2. Customize: Add your business name, address, or "APPROVED" text. Adjust the ink color and opacity.
  3. Upload: Click "Confirm & Proceed" and upload the PDF or image you want to stamp.
  4. Position: Drag, rotate, and resize the stamp anywhere on your document.
  5. Download: Export your stamped document as a secure, high-quality file.

Private and Secure Document Processing

We take your security seriously. Unlike other tools, Online Stamp Doc does not upload your files to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser. This means your sensitive business documents, contracts, and personal PDFs never leave your computer, providing 100% privacy.
