About Us

Our Mission

At Online Stamp Doc, our mission is to provide the world's most intuitive and powerful online stamp creation tool. We believe that adding official seals, custom badges, or personalized stamps to documents shouldn't require installing bulky, expensive software.

What We Do

Online Stamp Doc offers a suite of web-based tools designed to help individuals and businesses generate beautiful digital stamps. Whether you need a formal company seal, a multi-line address stamp, or a fun custom badge, our tool lets you build it in seconds directly inside your browser.

Privacy First

We built Online Stamp Doc with a privacy-first approach. All the heavy lifting—rendering the stamps and modifying PDFs—happens locally on your device. Your sensitive files never touch our servers, guaranteeing complete confidentiality.

How We Keep This Tool Free

To support hosting and ongoing improvements, this site may display advertisements (including Google AdSense). We do not sell your uploaded documents, and your files are not uploaded to our servers.
